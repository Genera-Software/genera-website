Welcome to your portal

Your dog's daycare,
in your pocket.

Everything you need to manage bookings, view photos, sign documents and pay invoices — all in one place. Follow the steps below to get set up.

Dog owners Customer portal Takes ~5 minutes
1
Get started

Your daycare sends you a unique invite link — check your inbox.

Your email inbox
Open your invite email
Your daycare sends you a personalised link. Click it to open your unique portal — the URL is specific to your daycare so keep it handy.
Portal → Set password
Set your password
Choose a secure password to protect your account. You'll use your email address and this password every time you log in.
Portal → My details
Fill in your details
Add your name, address and contact number. Your daycare uses this for emergencies, invoices and drop-off communications.
2
Add your dog

You can add multiple dogs to one account — repeat these steps for each.

Portal → Add a pet
Create your dog's profile
Name, breed, date of birth, photo — the basics. The daycare can see this the moment you save it.
Portal → Pet profile → Health
Add health & behaviour notes
Allergies, medication, behaviour around other dogs, vet contact details. The more you add, the better the daycare can care for your dog.
Portal → Pet profile → Vaccinations
Enter vaccination dates
Add your dog's vaccination records. You'll get automatic reminders before they expire — and so will the daycare, so nothing lapses.
Portal → Terms & conditions
Sign the daycare's T&Cs
Read and sign your daycare's terms digitally — no paper forms. You won't be able to request bookings until this step is complete.
3
Book a day

Request days directly from your dog's calendar — no calls or texts needed.

Portal → Your dog → Calendar
Pick a day on the calendar
Tap any available day on your dog's calendar to request it. Days shown as full are already at capacity — you can join the waitlist if your daycare has it enabled.
Portal → Booking request → Notes
Add a note for that day (optional)
Leaving early? Dog has a vet appointment later? Add a note specific to that booking — the daycare sees it in real time.
💡

What happens next? If your daycare has auto-accept on and there's space, your booking is confirmed immediately. If it needs manual approval, you'll get an email when the daycare accepts or declines — and if declined, they'll include a reason.

4
Day to day

Once you're set up, these are the things you'll use regularly.

Portal → Gallery
View your dog's photos
Your daycare uploads photos throughout the day. Check the gallery to see what your dog has been up to — new photos appear as soon as they're uploaded.
Portal → Documents
View daycare documents
Access your daycare's first-aid certificates, public liability insurance, packing lists and any other documents they've shared with you.
Portal → Invoices
View and pay invoices
See your billing history and pay outstanding invoices directly through the portal. Download PDF copies for your records any time.
